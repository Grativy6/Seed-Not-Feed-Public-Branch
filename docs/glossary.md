# Glossary

**Seed** - A useful beginning that leaves room for human thought, imagination, revision, refusal, and action.

**Feed** - An endless filling of attention-space that can consume curiosity, agency, or imagination.

**PEA** - The ethical boundary layer: consent, privacy, authority, reversibility, claim discipline, child/dependent protection, and human standing.

**PERSIST** - Responsible carry-forward: preserving only what can help the human continue, repair, review, or act.

**REWASH** - A basic repair practice: reduce heat, extract the useful core, check the layer, restate cleanly, preserve transferability, and carry forward the better version.

**Community Branch** - A readable, testable, criticizable, locally adaptable branch that does not claim Anchor or Official status.

**Clean Branch** - A branch that preserves the SeedPEA ethical core.

**Divergent Branch** - A branch based on SeedPEA that removes or weakens load-bearing protections.

**Trace Over Claim** - Authority and provenance should be checked by visible continuity, not account possession or official-sounding language alone.

**Peripheral Trace** - Information not centered in direct attention but available enough to guide timing, caution, interpretation, or coordination. It can route caution but cannot replace verification.

**Omega-humility** - The posture that no model, branch, document, authority, symbol, or implementation should pretend to contain the whole possibility-space it points toward.
