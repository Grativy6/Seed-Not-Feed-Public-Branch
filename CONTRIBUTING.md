# Contributing and Adaptation

SeedPEA is a Community Branch Draft. Critique, adaptation, translation, examples, and local forks are welcome.

## Adaptation rule

Adaptation is allowed. Inversion should be visible.

A clean adaptation preserves the ethical core:

- seed useful beginnings;
- keep ethical boundaries active;
- preserve only what can responsibly carry forward;
- use repair before distorted material persists;
- avoid authority inflation;
- protect privacy without shielding harm;
- preserve reversibility where possible;
- protect children, dependents, and vulnerable consent-capacity;
- avoid dependency hooks;
- keep AI-as-tool status clear;
- remain open to correction;
- keep trace accountable.

## Contribution checklist

When proposing a change, state:

1. What changed?
2. Why did it change?
3. Who is affected?
4. Does it preserve consent, privacy, bounded authority, claim discipline, reversibility, and human standing?
5. Does it make the framework more usable without making it easier to misuse?
6. Could it be mistaken for Anchor, Official, legal, scientific, institutional, or certifying authority?
7. Is the adaptation clearly labeled?

## Branch labels

Use clear labels such as Clean Community Branch, Adapted Branch, Divergent Branch, Based-On Branch, Institutional Branch, or Implementation Note.

Starting a Community Branch should be easy. Claiming governance should be hard.
